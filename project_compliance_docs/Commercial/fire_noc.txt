NO OBJECTION CERTIFICATE (NOC) - FIRE SAFETY
Issued by: Department of Fire & Emergency Services

NOC No: FES/NOC/2024/88
Date: 15-Jan-2024
Project: Skyline Business Center (Commercial High Rise)

Standard Operating Requirements:
1. Fixed Fire Fighting Systems:
   - Down-comer system: 100mm dia.
   - Size of Fire Pump: 900 LPM at terrace level.
   - Sprinklers: Mandatory for all floors and basements.
   - Hose Reels: Length 30m, near every staircase block.

2. Staircases:
   - Number of Staircases: 2 Nos. (Enclosed type)
   - Width: 1.50 meters clear width.
   - Pressurization: Required for lift lobbies and staircases.

3. Fire Alarms:
   - Smoke Detectors: Optical type in office spaces, Heat detectors in pantry.
   - Manual Call Points (MCP): At height 1.4m from FFL.
   - PA System: Integrated with fire alarm panel.

4. Evacuation Checks:
   - Maximum travel distance: 30m to nearest exit.
   - Refuge Area: Provided at 7th Floor (Height 24m).

Validity: 1 Year from date of issue. Renewable annually.
