TRAFFIC & PARKING MANAGEMENT PLAN
Project: Skyline Business Center

1. Parking Demand Calculation:
   - Total Built-up Area: 1,20,000 sq.ft
   - Commercial Norm: 1 ECS (Equivalent Car Space) per 500 sq.ft
   - Required Spots: 240 ECS

2. Proprosed Parking Layout:
   Basement Level 1:
   - Car Slots: 100 Nos
   - Two-Wheeler: 150 Nos
   - Ramp Width: 7.5m (Two way) slope 1:12

   Basement Level 2:
   - Car Slots: 120 Nos
   - Mechanical Stack Parking: 20 Nos
   - EV Charging Stations: 10 Bays (Fast Charging)

   Surface Parking:
   - Visitor slots: 25 Nos (Near entrance)
   - Drop-off Zone: Covered porch 6m wide.

3. Traffic Circulation:
   - Entry: Gate 1 (Left In)
   - Exit: Gate 1 (Left Out)
   - Internal Signage: Reflectorized directional arrows.
   - Pedestrian Walkways: 1.5m wide raised footpath along periphery.
