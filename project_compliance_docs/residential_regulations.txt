# RESIDENTIAL TOWNSHIP & APARTMENT REGULATIONS (2024)

## 1. ENVIRONMENTAL CLEARANCE (EC)
### 1.1 Thresholds
- **Category B2:** Built-up area ≥ 20,000 sq.m and < 1,50,000 sq.m. Requires EC from SEIAA.
- **Category B1:** Area ≥ 1,50,000 sq.m. Requires EIA study.

## 2. LANDSCAPING & GREEN COVER
### 2.1 Tree Plantation
- Minimum 1 tree for every 80 sq.m of plot area.
- Unpaved area must be at least 15% of total plot area to facilitate groundwater recharge.
- Native species preferred to minimize water consumption.

## 3. WATER SUSTAINABILITY
### 3.1 Rain Water Harvesting (RWH)
- Storage capacity design: 20 liters per sq.m of roof area.
- Recharge Pits: Minimum 2 pits for plot size > 500 sq.m.

### 3.2 Grey Water Recycling
- Dual Plumbing System mandatory for projects > 50 units.
- Recycled water must be used for flushing and gardening (Zero potable water for flushing).

## 4. BUILDING SETBACKS & OPEN SPACES
| Building Height (m) | Minimum Setback (m) |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| Up to 10m           | 3.0m                |
| 10m - 15m           | 5.0m                |
| 15m - 18m           | 6.0m                |
| Above 18m           | H/3 (Max 16m)       |

## 5. WASTE MANAGEMENT
- **Organic Waste Converter (OWC):** Mandatory for > 50 dwelling units.
- **Segregation:** Three-bin system at source (Bio-degradable, Non-bio, Domestic Hazardous).

## 6. AMENITIES
- **Solar Water Heating:** Mandatory for top 3 floors minimum.
- **Health:** Well-ventilated community hall/gym area required.

---
*Note: Violation of setback norms allows local bodies to demolish unauthorized extensions.*
