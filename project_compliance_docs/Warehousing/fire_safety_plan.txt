FIRE & STORAGE SAFETY PLAN
Project: FastTrack Logistics Hub

1. Classification of Hazard:
   - Block A: Ordinary Hazard (Carton boxes, FMCG).
   - Block B: High Hazard (Cold Storage - PUF Panels).

2. Fire Protection System (Planned):
   a) External Yard Hydrants:
      - Ring main surrounding entire facility.
      - Hydrants spaced at 45m intervals.
   
   b) Internal Protection:
      - Sprinkler Type: ESFR (Early Suppression Fast Response) - K14 Factor.
      - Design density: 30 L/min/sq.m for roof sprinklers.
      - In-Rack Sprinklers: Provided at every 3rd level in Block B.

   c) Detection:
      - Beam Detectors used for high-ceiling open areas.
      - VESDA (Very Early Smoke Detection) for Cold Storage.

3. Emergency Response:
   - Emergency Egress Doors: 6 Nos in Block A (Max travel distance 45m).
   - Assembly Point: Marked in North-East Truck Parking area.

4. Storage Rules:
   - Maximum stacking height: 10.5m
   - Aisle width: 3.2m (Reach truck operation).
   - Flammable liquids not permitted in General Warehouse.
