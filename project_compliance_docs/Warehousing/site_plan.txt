WAREHOUSE LOGISTICS HUB SITE PLAN
Project: FastTrack Logistics Hub
Location: NH-44, Logistics Park Sector 3
Total Site Area: 10 Acres (4,35,600 sq.ft)
Classification: Grade A - Modern Warehouse

Layout Specifications:
1. Warehouse Block A (FMCG): 50,000 sq.ft
   - Height: 12m clear height (for 7-high racking)
   - Flooring: Laser screed FM2 floor.
2. Warehouse Block B (Cold Storage): 25,000 sq.ft
   - Temperate Zones: +20C, +4C, -20C.
3. Truck Apron Area: 33m width (Concrete paved)
4. Admin & Canteen Block: 5,000 sq.ft

Docking Bays:
- Block A: 10 Dock Levelers (1.2m height)
- Block B: 4 Insulated Docks with Dock Shelters.

Entry/Exit:
- Main Gate: 18m wide (allowing parallel entry/exit of trailers).
- Weighbridge: 60 Ton class near entrance.

Fire Services:
- Fire Tank: 6 Lakh Liters Underground Tank (North Corner)
- Pump Room: Adjacent to tank.
