# COMMERCIAL COMPLEX BUILDING BYE-LAWS & REGULATIONS (2024)

## 1. FIRE & LIFE SAFETY
### 1.1 Fire Prevention
- **Fire Extinguishers:** One 5kg ABC type extinguisher for every 200 sq.m of floor area.
- **Hose Reels:** Located near every staircase, covering 30m radius.
- **Sprinkler System:** Mandatory for all basements and buildings > 15m height.
- **Fire Alarm:** Manual Call Points (MCP) and automatic smoke detectors in all corridors and common areas.

### 1.2 Egress & Evacuation
- **Staircase Width:** Minimum 1.50m for commercial buildings.
- **Travel Distance:** Maximum 30m from any point to the nearest exit.
- **Refuge Area:** Required at 24m height and every 15m thereafter. Area > 15 sq.m.

## 2. PARKING STANDARDS
### 2.1 Vehicle Parking Requirements
- **Commercial Offices:** 1 Car Space (ECS) per 50 sq.m of floor area.
- **Retail / Malls:** 1 ECS per 40 sq.m.
- **Visitors Parking:** Additional 10% of total required parking.
- **Electric Vehicle (EV) Charging:** Minimum 20% of parking slots must have EV charging infrastructure.

## 3. BUILDING ENVELOPE & ENERGY
### 3.1 ECBC Compliance
- **Glass Facade:** Solar Heat Gain Coefficient (SHGC) must be < 0.25.
- **Lighting Power Density:** Maximum 10.8 W/sq.m for interior office areas.
- **Rainwater Harvesting:** Mandatory recharge pits @ 1 per 500 sq.m of terrace area.

## 4. ACCESSIBILITY (DIVYANGJAN)
### 4.1 Ramps & Entrances
- **Ramp Gradient:** Max 1:12 slope with handrails on both sides.
- **Entrance:** At least one main entrance must be accessible (width > 1200mm).
- **Toilets:** At least one unisex accessible toilet on Ground Floor.

## 5. SANITATION & WASTE
- **Solid Waste:** Segregated application for Wet, Dry, and E-waste.
- **Sewage:** Connection to municipal line is mandatory. If unavailable, on-site STP required.

---
*Warning: Occupancy Certificate (OC) will be withheld if Fire NOC is not renewed annually.*
