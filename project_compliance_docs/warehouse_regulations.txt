# WAREHOUSE & LOGISTICS STORAGE REGULATIONS (2024)

## 1. STRUCTURAL SAFETY
### 1.1 Floor Load Capacity
- Industrial Grade Flooring (VDF) required.
- Minimum load bearing capacity: 5 Tonnes/sq.m.
- Anti-skid and dust-proof coating recommended.

### 1.2 Clear Heights
- Minimum clear height for pallet racking: 9m.
- Ventilation: Ridge ventilators required for air circulation.

## 2. FIRE SAFETY (HIGH HAZARD STORAGE)
### 2.1 Active Protection
- **Sprinklers:** Early Suppression Fast Response (ESFR) sprinklers mandatory for rack storage > 4m height.
- **Water Storage:** Dedicated fire tank capacity minimum 2,00,000 liters.
- **Hydrant Ring Main:** External hydrant system surrounding the building.

### 2.2 Compartmentation
- Maximum compartment size: 2500 sq.m per fire separation wall.
- Fire Walls: 4-hour fire rating, extending 1m above roof line.

## 3. LOADING & UNLOADING (DOCKING)
### 3.1 Dock Levelers
- One dock per 1000 sq.m of area.
- Dock height: 1.2m standard.
- Turning radius: Minimum 25m for 40ft trailers.

## 4. LIGHTING & ELECTRICAL
- **Lighting:** LED High bay lights providing 200 Lux in aisles, 300 Lux in staging areas.
- **Electrical Safety:** All wiring in FRLS (Fire Retardant Low Smoke) cables.
- **Battery Charging:** Separate ventilated room for forklift battery charging (Hydrogen gas hazard).

## 5. SECURITY & SURVEILLANCE
- CCTV Coverage: 100% coverage of entry/exit and loading bays.
- Perimeter Wall: Minimum 2.4m height with barbed wire fencing.

## 6. WORKER AMENITIES
- Separate restrooms for truck drivers and warehouse staff.
- Canteen/Rest area mandatory for facilities > 10,000 sq.ft.

---
*Specific compliance required for Cold Storage under Ammonia Safety Rules 1987.*
