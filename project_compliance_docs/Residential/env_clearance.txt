ENVIRONMENTAL CLEARANCE (EC)
Issued by: State Level Environment Impact Assessment Authority (SEIAA)
Category: B2 (Building & Construction)

Project: Harmony Gardens Township
Total Built-up Area: 45,000 sq.m (Requires EC)

Conditions for Compliance:
1. Water Usage:
   - Fresh Water Requirement: 135 LPCD reduced to 90 LPCD via low-flow fixtures.
   - Recycled Water: 100% of treated grey water must be used for flushing & gardening.
   - Release to Drain: Zero (During non-monsoon).

2. Energy Conservation:
   - Solar Water Heaters: Mandatory for top 3 floors of all blocks.
   - Solar PV: Minimum 5% of connected load for common areas lighting.
   - LED Lighting: 100% in common areas.

3. Air & Noise Environment:
   - DG Sets: Acoustic enclosure mandatory (Noise < 75 dB at 1m).
   - Construction Phase: Dust screens (6m height) around site periphery.

4. Green Cover:
   - Number of trees to be planted: 1 tree / 80 sq.m = Approx 275 trees.
   - Species: Native, broad-leaved species (Neem, Honge, etc).
