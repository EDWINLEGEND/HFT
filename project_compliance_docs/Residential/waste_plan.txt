SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT PLAN
Project: Harmony Gardens
Population: 135 households (~600 residents)
Estimated Waste Generation: 300 kg/day

1. Segregation at Source (Household Level):
   - Green Bin: Wet Waste (Kitchen scraps)
   - Blue Bin: Dry Waste (Paper, plastic, metal)
   - Red Bin: Hazardous/Sanitary waste

2. On-Site Processing (Wet Waste):
   - Technology: Organic Waste Converter (OWC) - Semi-Automatic
   - Capacity: 200 kg/batch
   - Location: Basement ramp designated area.
   - Output: Manure to be used for landscaping.

3. Dry Waste Handover:
   - Storage Room: 200 sq.ft area in Stilt floor.
   - Disposal: Weekly pickup by authorized municipal contractors / recyclers.

4. Reject Waste:
   - Inert material sent to Sanitary Landfill only (Target < 10%).
